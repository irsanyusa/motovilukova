This Is What I Pray For: Nigerian Man Marries Indian Lady, Video From Their Colourful Wedding

  • A colourful interracial wedding involving a Nigerian man and his Indian partner has got netizens gushing
  • Like in Bollywood movies, the lovebirds tied the knot in a lovely occasion patterned after the Asian country's tradition
  • Some social media users wondered how the lovebirds did it considering their different races, as they celebrated their union

A footage from the wedding of a Nigerian man and his Indian lover has gone viral on the social media platform TikTok.

The clip shared by @grakeshots on the platform has gathered over 600k views as netizens gush over the lovebirds.

Dressed in Indian attire, the lovebirds were joined as a couple in a colourful wedding like in Bollywood movies.

The bride had golden bracelets and was covered up save her face and palms.
