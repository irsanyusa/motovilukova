Emodee - 'Toriba' | Pulse Nigeria

After much wait, Godek Records finally presents ‘Toriba’ by Emodee.‘Toriba’ which means 'They can't do nothing'  was produced by Indiana. The joint is a perfect blend of High-life and Afro-pop, accompanied with a touch of classic music mastering. This is one song that will definitely keep you on your feet at first listen.
