Mukesh Ambani (Business Magnate) - On This Day

Full Name: Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani
Profession: Business Magnate

Biography: Mukesh Ambani is an Indian business tycoon known for his role as the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries, one of India's most significant conglomerates. As of 2024, he is the richest person in Asia and ranks 11th globally with a net worth of $112.1 billion.

Mukesh Ambani moved to India from Aden, Yemen, with his family in 1958. He grew up with his brother Anil Ambani and his two sisters in Mumbai. He started his education at Hill Grange High School, continued at St. Xavier's College for intermediate education, and earned a BE in Chemical Engineering from the Institute of Chemical Technology.

Ambani started an MBA at Stanford University but left the program in 1980 to help expand the family business, Reliance Industries, founded by his father.

Ambani's career at Reliance Industries began in 1981. He oversaw the construction of Patalganga, Hazira, and then the Jamnagar projects, which propelled Reliance Industries to heights of success.

He was instrumental in the creation of several new businesses including Reliance Infrastructure and Reliance Retail, and was a pioneer in the telecommunications space with the introduction of 4G services in India.

His contributions to engineering earned him election to the National Academy of Engineering in 2016. He has achieved recognition for his business acumen, consistently topping lists as India's richest person.

Despite his business successes, Ambani has been a central figure in numerous controversies. He has faced allegations ranging from market manipulation to political corruption. In 2014, an FIR was lodged against him related to natural gas pricing, and Reliance Industries was fined for market manipulation in 2007.

Ambani's activities outside of business include philanthropy and board memberships. Notably, he ranked fifth among India's philanthropists in 2015. His involvement in education and healthcare initiatives is also noteworthy. He is on the board of both the Institute of Chemical Technology and Reliance Industries.

On the personal front, Mukesh Ambani married Nita Ambani in 1985 and has three children. The family resides in Antilia, a skyscraper residence in Mumbai. He is known for his vegetarian diet and abstaining from alcohol.

Notably, in 2019, he aided his brother Anil Ambani by settling a debt owed to Ericsson. He was also involved in a bomb scare near his residence in 2021 and received a death threat via email in 2023.
