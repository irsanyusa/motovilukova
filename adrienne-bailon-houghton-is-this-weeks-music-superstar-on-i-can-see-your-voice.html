Adrienne Bailon-Houghton has been announced as the Music Superstar for this Wednesday’s new episode of I Can See Your Voice. Bailon-Houghton is a regular celebrity detective on the show. This week’s theme is Decades Night.

Adrienne Bailon-Houghton Is I Can See Your Voice Music Superstar

The concept of I Can See Your Voice, hosted by Ken Jeong, is that contestants must figure out if various people are good or bad singers without hearing them sing live. At the end of each episode, the last singer standing duets with a celebrity to reveal their voice.

This week, that celebrity is Adrienne Bailon-Houghton, who is a regular panelist on the show alongside Cheryl Hines. Bailon-Houghton is known for being a member of the girl groups 3LW and The Cheetah Girls. She also competed on The Masked Singer as Flamingo.

This week is Decades Night on I Can See Your Voice, with special guests including Frankie Muniz and Romeo Miller. A preview from Wednesday’s episode shows someone dressed as a zombie preparing to perform “My Heart Will Go On” from Titanic.

Next Week’s Theme Is Sports Night

After this week’s Decades Night, next Wednesday’s new episode of I Can See Your Voice will be Sports Night. The episode will feature Music Superstar Montell Jordan, as well as celebrity guest detectives Dwight Howard and Jon Lovitz.

The show is taking a page out of The Masked Singer‘s book this season with themed episodes. Speaking of The Masked Singer, I Can See Your Voice host Ken Jeong will be returning to the show as a panelist when Season 11 premieres March 6 on FOX.

Jeong will be joined by longtime panelists Robin Thicke and Jenny McCarthy. However, Nicole Scherzinger had to sit this season out, so Rita Ora will be filling in for her on the panel.
