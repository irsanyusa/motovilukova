The Masked Singer season 9 is bringing the star power and some serious vocal power. From Dick Van Dyke to Howie Mandel, it’s been a season of some of the biggest names yet. And this week, we meet three new performers, looking to take on California Roll and Medusa. One of these is the Wolf, who looks larger than life!

But which celebrity is under the mask? And what clues do we have about the Wolf? Let’s go through it all.

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 9

The Wolf teleported onto the stage before we saw his clues. They started with him in an icy cave saying “welcome to my love den” with a candelabra behind him. We see a framed photo of Andy Garcia with a heart around it and a pirate ship. Then, Wolf whips out a saxophone and starts playing it “to set the mood.” He’s a “sucker for romance,” he says while petting the Leopard’s head (who was revealed to be Seal).

Next, we see a clock with no hands. He claims he’s “sure [he’s] responsible for more than one baby out there” while we see him drinking from a champagne flute with a yellow drink and cherry in it. The camera then pans to a framed photo of Nick Cannon with “HERO” written on it. Wolf is seen lying in a round bed playing with the pirate ship, then gets a VHS handed to him with “The Wolf” on it. We then see a framed photo and he mentions his “friends,” Lady Gaga, Jay-Z, and Andy Samberg.

The last clue came from Zachary Levi and Dame Helen Mirren after Wolf’s performance. It was “Timberlake,” which Wolf explained saying, “Here’s a thought: JT and I collaborated.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Wolf

Robin (as a self-proclaimed soulful white boy) predicted that the Wolf is Michael Bolton, which Jenny seems to agree with. But Ken thought Robin was wrong, instead guessing Richard Marx. Nicole wasn’t buying it though, predicting it could be Rico Suave…just kidding! She guessed Michael Bolton too. Seems like the judges, and audience online, all agree on this one.

Who is the Wolf on The Masked Singer?

After the first performances, the Wolf was the one who got eliminated! And he was revealed to be…

Michael Bolton!

We genuinely can’t believe that such an iconic, Grammy Award-winner singer left so early. DC Superheroes Night was quite the stacked cast! But how will the Squirrel and the Gargoyle fare in the next round? Tune in to The Masked Singer on Wednesdays to find out!
