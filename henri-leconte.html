Henri Leconte (Tennis Player) - On This Day

Profession: Tennis Player

Biography: Best known for reaching the men's singles final at the French Open in 1988. His career-high singles ranking was World No. 5.

Born: July 4, 1963
Birthplace: Lillers, France
Age: 61 years old

Generation: Baby Boomer
Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
Star Sign: Cancer


Playing Career
