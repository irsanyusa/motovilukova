How to Dress Like Your Favorite Schitts Creek Character

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Though “Schitt’s Creek” is sadly over, the beloved series somehow seems more popular than ever. After all, after six seasons, it swept the Emmys, winning outstanding comedy series and acting prizes for all four leads. That’s proof that while there may not be any new episodes, “Schitt’s Creek” will live on forever in our hearts, TVs and closets.

There are so many things to love about “Schitt’s Creek” and each character’s unique style tops the list. Though the Rose family lost all of their money, they didn’t lose their wardrobes. Each family member had incredible, distinct personal style that captivated audiences as much as their personalities. We rounded up picks that seem right out of the wardrobes of your favorite “Schitt’s Creek” characters, so you can add a little Moira Rose to your jewelry box, an Alexis Rose-approved hair accessory, Johnny Rose-style pajamas or a pair of sneakers that David Rose would take a spin in. Dress like your favorite “Schitt’s Creek” character by shopping their signature looks.
