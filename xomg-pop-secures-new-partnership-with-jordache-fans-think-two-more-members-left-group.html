XOMG POP! Secures New Partnership With Jordache

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The latest developments surrounding rising pop sensation XOMG POP! has been nothing short of intriguing. The group recently announced a groundbreaking partnership, while also facing rumors about members departing the group. Fans of XOMG POP! are uncertain as to why group members continue to leave the girl group.

XOMG POP! Partners With Denim Brand, Jordache

XOMG POP! recently announced their partnership with Jordache, an iconic denim brand inspired by the fashion trends of the 80s. This brand has embarked on a fresh journey by joining hands with XOMG POP! for an exciting back-to-school campaign. This partnership serves as a bridge between Jordache’s rich legacy and the contemporary flair exuded by the rising pop stars.

The members of XOMG POP! have handpicked ensembles from Jordache’s diverse lineup. Reflecting their individual fashion preferences and creating a harmonious blend of retro nostalgia and contemporary aesthetics.

From classic jeans that pay homage to Jordache’s historical influence to modern jackets and overalls that merge style and comfort seamlessly. The collaboration showcases the group’s ability to infuse their own spirit into fashion. This curated collection is now available for purchase on Walmart.com.

Furthermore, it allows fans and fashion enthusiasts alike to infuse their back-to-school wardrobes with a mix of nostalgia and the exciting allure of XOMG POP! music. The partnership between Jordache and XOMG POP! signifies more than just a collaboration; it represents the convergence of music and fashion that resonates across generations.

Fans Speculate Two More Members Left The Group After Recent Posts From XOMG POP!

While the partnership with Jordache brings a fresh spark to XOMG POP!’s journey, the group is also having its share of challenges in terms of its lineup. Recent social media activity on the group’s official Instagram page has also raised concerns among fans as only three out of the five members, namely Brooklynn Pitts, Dallas Skye, and Tinie T, have been featured in their recent posts. Notably absent from the recent posts are Bella Cianni Llerena and Kinley Cunningham.

This development follows the departure of both Kiya Barczyszyn and Leigha Sanderson in late 2022 and early 2023, respectively. While the group had managed to adapt and continue with their music despite the initial departures, the recent absence of two more members has sparked speculations and concerns among fans.

The comment section on one of XOMG POP!’s recent posts has been flooded with reactions from fans expressing their shock, disappointment, and curiosity. Many fans are calling for transparency and also an explanation from the group regarding the changes. Some speculate that there might be behind-the-scenes issues leading to these departures. While others express their disappointment in the lack of communication.
