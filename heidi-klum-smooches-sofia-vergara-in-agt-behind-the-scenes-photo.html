Heidi Klum and Sofia Vergara have recently been posting photos to Instagram from their time filming Season 19 of America’s Got Talent. Klum and Vergara are returning to the judging table alongside Howie Mandel and Simon Cowell. In one shot, Klum can be seen giving Vergara a smooch on set.

Heidi Klum Kisses Sofia Vergara on the AGT Set

AGT is currently filming auditions for Season 19 of the show, set to premiere in May on NBC. The judges have been sharing photos from their time on set, as they decide which acts should move on to the next round — and maybe even earn the Golden Buzzer.

In one photo shared by both Klum and Vergara on Instagram, the two judges can be seen making kissy faces at each other from their place at the judging table, with the audience behind them. Vergara captioned the photo, “Amigas!!” She added a few red heart emojis.

Klum also shared the photo, writing in the caption, “Are you missing me today @sofiavergara.” The judges may have taken a day off from auditions, but they were back to work earlier this week. Klum shared a quick clip from a night shoot, in which the judges sat outside.

Season 19 Will Premiere in May

AGT will return for Season 19 with a premiere on Tuesday, May 28, 2024. All four judges from last season will be returning to the panel this season, along with host Terry Crews. Vergara recently sat out the spin-off show Fantasy League, with Mel B judging instead.

The show is scheduled to tape episodes of the new season into April, with free tickets available to fans through On Camera Audiences. As usual, the prize for winning the show includes $1 million. Last season, dog act Adrian Stoica & Hurricane took home the prize — who will be next?
