Cristiano Ronaldo Reveals the Real Reason Why He Still Lives With His Mom

A reporter once asked Cristiano Ronaldo why he still lives with his mother despite his success and millions of dollars. While some might assume that he would have moved on to more extravagant living arrangements, Ronaldo’s response is truly touching and may bring a tear to your eye.

Cristiano Ronaldo’s family lived in poverty and faced financial difficulties.

Cristiano Ronaldo’s profound admiration and love for his mother, Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro, is truly heartrending. He witnessed firsthand the sacrifices and dedication she made while raising him, and he never forgets the importance of their relationship.

As a single mother, Maria Aveiro worked tirelessly to provide for her family. She often went without food and worked long hours to ensure that Ronaldo never needed anything. Ronaldo is acutely aware of the sacrifices his mother made for him, and he acknowledges that he wouldn’t be where he is today without her constant support, guidance, and hard work.

For Ronaldo, his mother is not just a parent, but a hero and a role model.

Ronaldo went on, explaining his decision: “My mother reared me up by sacrificing her life for me. She went to bed hungry to feed me. We had no money. She worked 7 days a week and worked nights as a cleaner to buy me a pair of [soccer] boots, so I could play.”

Ronaldo holds his mother in the highest regard, and he credits his success to her support and sacrifices. When asked about their relationship, he expressed that his whole life’s achievements are dedicated to her. He plans to continue providing her with everything he can give her.

“Sometimes Ronaldo makes fun of me and says, ’You didn’t want me to be born. But now you see that I’m here helping all of you,’” said his mom.

And his appreciation for his mother goes on for miles.

Cristiano Ronaldo had another beautiful moment with his mother when he took to social media to wish her a happy birthday. The famous soccer player shared a photo of his mom (the pic below) and wrote a touching caption, “Happy Birthday to the best mother in the world, a warrior who always taught me not to give up, the best grandmother my children could ever dream of having. I love you so much, my mother! Kiss!”

Today, Ronaldo is not only a global icon but also the highest-paid soccer player in the world, playing for Al-Nassr. His success is all due to the power of hard work, dedication, and the love and support of a mother who never stopped believing in her child. Ronaldo knows that he owes everything to his mother who sacrificed so much to give him the opportunity to chase his dreams. And he will always be grateful for her love and unconditional support.
