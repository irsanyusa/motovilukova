How we selected the best beauty Advent calendars

There are a lot of beauty Advent calendars out there this year—to narrow down the options, we researched the top concerns shoppers had when making these purchases. We considered things like giftability, value of the products, overall design, and ease of ordering.

What you should know before buying beauty Advent calendars

Number of days

Some Advent calendars contain 24 (sometimes even 25) days (starting on December 1st), and others have only 12 days. It will depend on the brand or retailer and how they promote their products. For example, beauty Advent calendars with full-sized products may only contain 12 days.

Delivery speed

If you want to do the full 24 days, you’ll want to make sure the Advent calendar arrives before December 1.

Types of beauty Advent calendars

Most beauty Advent calendars will have a mix of full and trial-sized products for you to try out. With so many beauty brands on the Advent calendar bandwagon, you can likely find specialized calendars from particular brands or general ones full of a variety of products and makers. You can buy them directly from the brands, online shops, or look for them in department stores. There are endless combinations of beauty Advent calendars from skincare and makeup, to fragrances and lip gloss. The larger calendars usually contain an assortment of items, so you don’t end up with too many of one product.

What’s inside

Consider the products you like when looking for the best beauty Advent calendars. If you prefer perfume, you may want to stick with a calendar that will gift you sweet smells, whereas a makeup calendar could provide shimmer and shine for the holidays. If you plan to have guests, you could choose a beauty Advent calendar with mini soaps or hand creams and leave them out for your friends and family who come to stay.

Who are you buying for?

You can find beauty Advent calendars for all different genders and ages. Due to their popularity, beauty Advent calendars come in many packages, shapes, and sizes, from ornate affairs to casual cardboard. If you are buying an Advent calendar as a gift, it might be worth making sure the recipient isn’t allergic to any of the products in it.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Are beauty Advent calendars worth it?

Beauty Advent calendars are a way to try best-selling and new products from your favorite brands. Sometimes, “gifts” hidden within the Advent calendars would cost more if you bought them individually. Beauty Advent calendars are worth it if you love beauty products and don’t mind samples or surprises. The gifts inside can make great stocking stuffers, and the calendars are often pretty enough to be given as gifts or used as decor.

What is the most popular beauty Advent calendar?

There really isn’t just one popular beauty Advent calendar on the market, since popularity depends on the category or brand a shopper is most interested in. In fact, there are several that consumers love and return to year after year. However, a few stand out as popular picks—L’Occitane, Sephora, Ulta, Kiehl’s, and Trader Joe’s.

What is the most luxurious Advent calendar?

Many luxurious Advent calendars are on the market in 2023, including ones from Liberty, Neiman Marcus, Diptyque, and more.
