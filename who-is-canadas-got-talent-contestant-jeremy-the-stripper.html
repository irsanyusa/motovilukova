The revival of Canada’s Got Talent is coming up soon, and we’re learning more about what types of acts we can expect to see. One of those acts is Jeremy the Stripper, who posted on Instagram about his upcoming appearance on the show.

Jeremy the Stripper to Appear on ‘Canada’s Got Talent’

Jeremy recently took to his Instagram account and posted a shot of himself in a new commercial for the series. He can be seen wearing a pair of red underwear and not much else. As you can probably guess from his name, Jeremy is a stripper.

The contestant owns a male strip club in Toronto called Klub Kave. He posted a video earlier this week promoting “Ladies Night” at the club on Fridays and Saturdays. The clip looks straight out of Magic Mike, with the club’s dancers performing with chairs and cowboy hats as accessories.

Based on other clips from his social media, Jeremy will be showing off some impressive moves on the Got Talent stage. In one video posted last month, he lifts a woman into the air and quickly flips her over so her legs are over his shoulders.

“Just an other day in the life of Jeremy The Stripper,” he wrote in the caption.

In another video posted to Instagram, Jeremy pours candle wax onto his body. It makes us wonder if he’ll perform this trick during his CGT audition.

The New Season Premieres March 22

You’ll be able to see Jeremy the Stripper and other acts perform during the new season of Canada’s Got Talent, premiering March 22 on Citytv. The show originally aired in 2012 but was canceled for 10 years before this year’s revivial.

The show will be hosted by singer Lindsay Ell, with America’s Got Talent judge Howie Mandel sitting on the judging panel, alongside comedian Lilly Singh, rapper Kardinal Offishall, and wrestler Trish Stratus.

Considering how many of Jeremy’s moves involve other people, we wouldn’t be surprised if he invites one of the judges on stage to help him with his routine.
