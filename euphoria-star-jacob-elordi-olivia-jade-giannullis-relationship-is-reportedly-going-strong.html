'Euphoria' Star Jacob Elordi, Olivia Jade Giannulli's Relationship is Reportedly 'Going Strong'

Love knows no boundaries, and when it blossoms amid the glitz and glamour of Hollywood and the digital realm, it captures the hearts of many. Recently, the spotlight has been shining on the enchanting romance between Jacob Elordi, the brilliant star of the hit series Euphoria, and Olivia Jade Giannulli, the captivating influencer renowned for her online presence. After a series of ups and downs in their relationship, the couple is now reportedly “getting serious,” much to the excitement of their fans.

Jacob Elordi, Olivia Jade Giannulli Are ‘Getting Serious’ in Their Relationship

Their journey of love and rediscovery took them on an intimate vacation to Italy in early June, where they basked in the sun on the beautiful beaches of Paraggi, near Portofino. The world witnessed their affectionate moments, and it was clear that their bond was rekindling. Later in July, the couple ventured to Lake Coeur d’Alene, Idaho, for a delightful holiday with Giannulli’s parents, the esteemed actress Lori Loughlin and the talented fashion designer Mossimo Giannulli.

Although Elordi had to depart early to Australia due to work commitments, they filled the time they spent together in Idaho with joy and happiness. Observers couldn’t help but gush about the couple, describing them as “a really cute couple” who radiated genuine affection for each other. Their togetherness is a testament to their commitment and growing love.

While their relationship journey has been a subject of interest, it was in late 2021 that the spark between Elordi and Giannulli first ignited. After being spotted together, getting coffee in Los Angeles, rumors of a romance began to swirl. At the time, insiders shared varying accounts, with some sources suggesting they were “casually dating” while others disagreed.

In the months that followed, their connection endured, and after a brief separation, they decided to give their love another chance. Their affection for each other became evident as they were photographed together at various events and outings.

Both Have Successful Careers in Entertainment

As their romance continues to flourish, the couple has become more open about their feelings for each other. A source close to them confirmed that they are now “100 percent going strong,” signaling a deeper commitment to their relationship.

While Elordi’s fame skyrocketed with his role in the 2018 hit movie The Kissing Booth, he is not resting on his laurels. The actor is gearing up to portray the iconic Elvis Presley in Sofia Coppola’s upcoming biopic Priscilla. In a candid interview, Elordi expressed his admiration for Elvis and his multifaceted talents, showing a genuine desire to learn and grow as an actor.

Amidst their individual successes, Elordi and Giannulli find solace and joy in each other’s company. Their love story reminds us that even in the world of showbiz and influencers, real connections can form and thrive. As they embrace the adventure of love, their fans eagerly await the unfolding of their next chapters together.
