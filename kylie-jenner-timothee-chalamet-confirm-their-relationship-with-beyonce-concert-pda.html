After months of speculation, undeniable proof has been given, Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet are officially an item. The world watched as the rumored couple made their first public appearance at Beyoncé’s star-studded Renaissance World Tour concert on September 4.

Kylie Jenner, Timothée Chalamet Make Their First Public Appearance as a Couple

The Jenner Cosmetics mogul, 26, and the Oscar-nominated actor, 27, have been at the center of rumors since the beginning of the year. However, they have carefully guarded their relationship from prying eyes, keeping their blossoming love affair largely under wraps, until now.

The electrifying night out at SoFi Stadium marked a significant milestone for the couple and solidified their status as Hollywood’s hottest new pairing. As they strolled into the VIP section hand in hand, with Kendall Jenner, the world couldn’t help but take notice.

The concert was a star-studded affair, attended by tons of people in entertainment industry. This included Jenner’s siblings Kim Kardashian and Khloe Kardashian, who brought along their kids, North West and Penelope Disick. Jenner’s mother Kris Jenner, and her beau, Corey Gamble, were also present. This crowd indicated that Chalamet has comfortably integrated into Jenner’s inner circle.

This high-profile appearance at Beyoncé’s concert leaves no doubt that Jenner and Chalamet are ready to embrace their status as a new couple. This new romance is captivating fans with its undeniable chemistry on full display.

Fans Who Thought They Broke Up Were Proven Wrong

Rumors of a possible breakup that swirled in August have been swiftly dispelled by this conspicuous display of togetherness. In fact, sources even captured footage of the couple sharing an intimate kiss while Beyoncé belted out her hit, “Single Ladies.” The video left no room for doubt, Jenner and Chalamet are very much an item.

Prior to Chalamet, Jenner was in a long-term relationship with rapper Travis Scott. Jenner and Scott share two children, five-year-old Stormi, and Aire, 19 months. While the couple has parted ways romantically, they have maintained an amicable co-parenting relationship, putting their children’s well-being first.

On the other hand, Chalamet’s dating history has included a high-profile romance with actress Lily-Rose Depp in 2019, though the two have since gone their separate ways. Now, all eyes are on his red-hot connection with Jenner.
